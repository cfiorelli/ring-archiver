Ring Video Archiver

Download and keep your Ring camera videos before Ring deletes them. Runs entirely on your own computer — your videos and password never go anywhere except to Ring.

Step 1 · Download for your computer
Other computers
One-time safety prompt: because this is a small free tool, your computer may warn you the first time.
• Mac: right-click the downloaded app → Open → Open again.
• Windows: click More info → Run anyway.
Step 2 · Use it
  1. Open the app — your web browser opens to a sign-in page.
  2. Sign in with your Ring email & password; type the 6-digit code Ring texts you.
  3. Pick your camera, click Download videos, and walk away.

Videos save oldest first (those expire soonest) into a Ring Videos folder. You can close and reopen anytime — it skips anything already saved.
